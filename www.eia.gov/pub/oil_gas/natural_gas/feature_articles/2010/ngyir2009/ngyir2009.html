Market Digest:  Natural Gas (2013-2014)

Updated: June 12, 2014  

 

 

 

For prior report data see Natural Gas Year-in-Review archives

EIA's Natural Gas Market Digest highlights the latest information and analyses on all aspects of the natural gas markets.

Storage

  • Record winter withdrawals create summer storage challenges, June 12, 2014
  • Natural gas storage working capacity grows 2% in 2012 — Today in Energy, July 24, 2013
  • High natural gas inventory last spring limited injections during the 2012 storage injection season —  Natural Gas Weekly Update, January 24, 2013

Pipelines

  • High prices show stresses in New England natural gas delivery system, February 7, 2014
  • Gas pipeline expansions reduce Marcellus backup, New York gas prices — Natural Gas Weekly Update, November 13, 2013
  • Marcellus gas pipe capacity seen rising 0.5 Bcf/d by month's end; additional expansions expected this winter — Natural Gas Weekly Update, September 19, 2013
  • Decreased Canadian Mainline gas flows constrain prices in western Canada, Pacific Northwest — Natural Gas Weekly Update, August 29, 2013

Production

  • Natural gas production increases by 1% in 2013, January 16, 2014
  • Increased Northeast natural gas production reduces net inflow of supply from other areas — Today in Energy, November 19, 2013
  • Production of fossil fuel from federal and Indian lands fell in 2012 — Today in Energy, August 12, 2013

Prices

  • Natural gas prices reflect decreasing seasonality — Today in Energy, November 20, 2013
  • Low natural gas prices drive industrial natural gas consumption — Natural Gas Weekly Update, September 5, 2013
  • Natural gas liquids prices trend down since the start of 2012 — Today in Energy, July 29, 2013
  • Low natural gas prices in 2012 reduced returns for some oil and gas producers — Today in Energy, May 29, 2013
  • 2012 Brief: Natural gas liquids prices down in 2012 — Today in Energy, January 15, 2013
  • 2012 Brief: Average wholesale natural gas prices fell 31% in 2012 — Today in Energy, January 8, 2013

Consumption

  • EIA projects lower natural gas use this winter — Natural Gas Weekly Update, October 31, 2013
  • Natural gas generation lower than last year because of differences in relative fuel prices — Today in Energy, September 25, 2013
  • REX gas deliveries to the Northeast fall as Appalachian production grows — Natural Gas Weekly Update, September 12, 2013
  • Fundamental natural gas market changes become apparent as winter temperatures return to 10-year average levels — Natural Gas Weekly Update, June 6, 2013

Imports and exports

  • Northeast net imports from Canada plummet, driven by export growth at Niagara Falls — Natural Gas Weekly Update, October 10, 2013
  • Net imports were at the lowest levels since 1992 — Natural Gas Imports and Exports 2012, July 22, 2013

Disruptions

  • Gulf of Mexico Fact Sheet updated and now includes energy infrastructure maps with real-time storm information, July 1, 2013
  • EIA's estimates of storm-related production disruptions in U.S. Gulf of Mexico during the 2013 hurricane season — Short Term Energy Outlook, June 11, 2013
