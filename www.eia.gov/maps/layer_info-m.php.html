    Federal Lands Federally owned or administered lands of the United States, Puerto Rico, and the U.S. Virgin Islands. CLASS 999 regions have temperatures less than 150° C at 10 km depth and were not assessed for deep EGS potential. Temperature at depth data for deep EGS in Alaska and Hawaii not available. National Renewable Energy Laboratory (NREL).

    Indian Lands Indian lands of the United States. For the most part, only areas of 320 acres or more are included; some smaller areas deemed to be important or significant are also included. Some established Indian lands which are larger than 320 acres are not included in this map layer because their boundaries were not available from the owning or administering agency. United States Department of the Interior, National Atlas. As of Dec. 2014 9/1/2015 - DFGPSU

EIA, Federal Energy Regulatory Commission, and U.S. Dept. of Transportation 2016 7/7/2016 Shapefile DFGPSU
    Natural Gas Interstate and Intrastate Pipelines Natural gas interstate and intrastate pipelines in the United States. Collected by EIA from FERC and other external sources. June 2016 7/7/2016 Shapefile DGPSU

EIA Varies, 1996-2009 7/1/2013 Shapefile DGSU

    Solar Resources Global Horizontal Irradiance (Physcial Solar Model) annual average that is averaged over surface cells of 0.038 degrees in both latitude and longitude, or nominally 4 km in size. The solar radiation values represent the resource available to solar energy systems. The data are averaged from hourly model output over 17 years (1998-2014). National Renewable Energy Laboratory (NREL), National Solar Radiation Database (NSRDB) 1998-2014 average 7/7/2016 - DGSU
    Solid Biomass Resources Biomass resources available in the United States by county.
