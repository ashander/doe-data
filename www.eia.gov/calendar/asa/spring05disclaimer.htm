In two adjacent files you may find unedited transcripts of EIA’s spring 2005 meeting with the American Statistical Association Committee on Energy Statistics. Beginning with the fall 2003 meeting, EIA no longer edits these transcripts.

Summaries of previous meetings may be found to the right of the Thursday and Friday transcripts, here at: http://www.eia.gov/calendar/asa_overview.htm

The public meeting took place April 28 and 29, 2005 in the Forrestal Building at 1000 Independence Ave., S.W., Washington, D.C. 20585. All of the plenary and four of the break-out sessions were in 8E-089. The five remaining break-out sessions were in 5E-069.

The spring meeting agenda, papers, presentation slides and other materials may be bound at: http://www.eia.gov/smg/asa_meeting_2005/spring/index.html
