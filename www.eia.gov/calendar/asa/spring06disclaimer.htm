In two adjacent files you may find unedited transcripts of EIA’s spring 2006 meeting with the American Statistical Association Committee on Energy Statistics. Beginning with the fall 2003 meeting, EIA no longer edits these transcripts.

Summaries of previous meetings may be found to the right of the Thursday and Friday transcripts.

The public meeting took place April 6 and 7, 2006 in the Forrestal Building at 1000 Independence Ave., S.W., Washington, D.C. 20585. All of the plenary and three of the break-out sessions were in 7E-069. The three remaining break-out sessions were in 5E-069.

The fall meeting agenda, papers, presentation slides and other materials may be bound at: http://www.eia.gov/smg/asa_meeting_2006/spring/index.html
