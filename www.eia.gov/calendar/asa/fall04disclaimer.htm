In an adjacent file you may find an unedited transcript of EIA’s Fall 2004 meeting with the American Statistical Association Committee on Energy Statistics. Beginning with the fall 2003 meeting, EIA no longer edits these transcripts.

The public meeting took place October 28 and 29, 2004 in the Forrestal Building at 1000 Independence Ave., S.W., Washington, D.C. 20585. All of the plenary and five of the break-out sessions were in 8E-089. The five remaining break-out sessions were in 5E-069.

Questions may be addressed to Bill Weinig, EI-70, at (202) 287-1709. Bill is EIA' s Liaison with the ASA Committee on Energy Statistics, and may also be reached by email at william.weinig@eia.doe.gov.
