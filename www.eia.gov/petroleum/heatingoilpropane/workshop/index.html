2016 State Heating Oil and Propane Program (SHOPP) Workshop

U.S. Department of Energy Forrestal Building
1000 Independence Avenue SW Washington, DC 20585
Room GH-027

Wednesday, July 13, 2016
9:00 am - 3:15 pm
Time Workshop agenda
8:00 - 8:45 am U.S. Department of Energy Visitor Registration**
Please see required forms of identification
8:30 - 9:15 am Light breakfast and coffee
9:15 - 9:20 am Welcome - Adam Sieminski, Administrator, U.S. Energy Information Administration
9:20 - 9:45 am Biofuel Requirements in Diesel and Heating Oil - Tony Radich, USDA
9:45 - 10:00 am Questions and Answers
10:00 - 10:30 am Propane Use in Agriculture - Mike Caldarera, NPGA
10:30 - 10:45 am Break
10:45 - 11:15 am Densified Biomass Fuel Survey
Massachusetts Wood Pellet Survey - Channele Wirman, EIA, and Karin Pisiewski, Massachusetts DOER
11:15 - 11:30 am Questions and Answers
11:30 - 12:15 pm U.S. Heating Fuels Market Outlook - Sean Hill, Tyler Hodge, Stacy MacIntyre, Katie Teller, EIA
12:15 - 1:30 pm Lunch - Forrestal Cafeteria (not provided, cash, debit, or credit accepted)
1:30 - 2:15 pm SHOPP: Program Discussion - Marcela Rourk, EIA
SHOPP Survey Statistical Methodology
Presentation of EIA Resources Available to States
Discussion of Improvements to SHOPP
2:15 - 3:00 pm SHOPP: Program and Data Quality Discussion - David Dudley and Hallie Black, EIA
3:00 - 3:15 pm Questions and Answers


Questions about the workshop?
