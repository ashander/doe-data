Coal Transportation Rate Database            

The Coal Transportation Rate Database

This Coal Transportation Rate Database (CTRDB) adds new data for 2000 and 2001.  The Federal Energy Regulatory Commission's (FERC) Form 580 "interrogatories" are the primary source for the CTRD.  The FERC Form 580 receives data for coal deliveries to non-jurisdictional utilities, concentrated in several States.  In those areas, the CTRDB is supplemented with general rate, distance, and tonnage data from the Department of Transportation, Surface Transportation Board (STB), based on waybill samples.  Confidential transportation rate or mine rate data (f.o.b. mine value) are entered as zeroes in the database. Confidential rate data are withheld upon respondent request throughout the CTRDB per FERC and STB policy to protect sensitive data whose disclosure may cause competitive harm.

In order to facilitate downloading and processing, the CTRDB is saved as two compatible Excel file modules--one containing all data for the years 1979 through 1992 and the other all data for 1993 through 2001. You can access the data by downloading the zip files. The zip files contain the CTRDB modules in Microsoft Excel spreadsheets and a Readme file in Microsoft Word.  Each module requires approximately 13 MB of space.
