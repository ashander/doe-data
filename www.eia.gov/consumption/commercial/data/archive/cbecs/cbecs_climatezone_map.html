Corrections to 2003 Climate Zone Map (February 2012)


Arizona: The numbers on the state map shown here represent the NOAA climate divisions. The previous version
of the CBECS climate zone map incorrectly placed climate divisions 3, 4, and 7 into CBECS climate zone 3 (yellow).
These three climate divisions are in CBECS climate zone 4 (orange), as shown on the corrected version of the map.


South Carolina: The numbers on the state map shown here represent the NOAA climate divisions.
The previous version of the CBECS climate zone map incorrectly placed climate division 1 into CBECS
climate zone 4 (orange). This climate division is in CBECS
climate zone 3 (yellow), as shown on the corrected version of the map.
