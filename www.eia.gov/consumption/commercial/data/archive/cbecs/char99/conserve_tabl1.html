The conservation features and practices categories are found as rowstubs in most detailed tables. Total buildings, total floorspace, and average building size for these categories are shown in Table B1.

The PDF and spreadsheet data tables are accompanied by tables of relative standard errors (RSEs). The RSE is a measure of the accuracy of the sample data and can be used to construct confidence ranges for the data.
