Corrections to 1979-1999 CBECS Climate Zone Map, February 2012

Arizona: The numbers on the state map shown here represent the NOAA climate divisions. The previous version
of the CBECS climate zone map incorrectly placed climate divisions 3, 4, and 7 into CBECS climate zone 3 (yellow).
These three climate divisions are in CBECS climate zone 4 (orange), as shown on the corrected version of the map.
