   FOOD SERVICE BUILDINGS
Where are they located?

Food service buildings are distributed fairly similar to population among the Census regions, with just a slightly lower percent of food service buildings than population in the Northeast and a slightly higher percent in the South. 

Food service buildings in the Midwest are larger than those in the rest of the country.
