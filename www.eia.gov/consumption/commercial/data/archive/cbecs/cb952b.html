1995 Detailed Tables

The following 44 tables contain estimates of number of buildings and square footage by selected commercial buildings characteristics. These data are from the Buildings Characteristics Survey of the 1995 CBECS. The "Quick-Reference Guide," indicates the major topics of each table. For a discussion of the organization of the tables and statistical significance of data.
