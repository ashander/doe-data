1995 Detailed Tables
Data from the 1995 Commercial Buildings Energy Consumption Survey (CBECS) are presented in three groups of detailed tables:

Buildings Characteristics Tables, number of buildings and amount of floorspace for major building characteristics.
Energy Consumption and Expenditures Tables, energy consumption and expenditures for major energy sources.
Energy End-Use Data, total, electricity and natural gas consumption and energy intensities for nine specific end-uses.
Summary Table—All Principal Buildings Activities (HTML Format)
