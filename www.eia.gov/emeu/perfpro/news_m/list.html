 Company List for the Financial News for Major Energy Producers

Links to the press releases of 21 companies are provided below, which is the number of companies that are included in this report.   Only 20 of the companies are included in compilation of corporate revenue and corporate net income because the U.S. operations of BP are included in the results of the U.S. lines of business, but not in the foreign or corporate results because it is foreign based and does not report the overall revenues and net income of its U.S. affiliate.

Note, that several changes in the cast of companies have occurred recently. In particular, Royal Dutch Shell stopped reporting U.S. line of business information with the third quarter of 2009 and consequently was removed from the historical data to maintain year-to-year comparability of the data. Additionally, Alon USA and Western Refining were added beginning with the first quarter of 2010, including data from earlier earnings releases for over the period Q404 through Q409. These two companies were added to reflect their addition to the set of respondent companies to Form EIA-28 (Financial Reporting System) for the 2009 reporting year.

The Financial Reporting System reporting criteria are 1 percent, or more, of U.S. crude oil and natural gas liquids, or natural gas production, or 1 percent, or more, of U.S. refinery crude oil distillation capacity.
